> **Offensive security tools for those who need it most.**

We build open-source security tools designed for the frontlines — where surveillance is real, infrastructure is unreliable, and the stakes are high. No corporate fluff. No compromise.

---

## ⚡ Our Projects

### 🔗 [HavenLink](https://github.com/RebelGuard/havenlink)
A secure peer-to-peer mesh chat system built for high-risk users. Operates over low-bandwidth **LoRa radio links** for off-grid comms, or over **internet/Tor** for global reach with IP privacy. No central servers. No single point of failure.

> *For when you need to communicate and the infrastructure is gone — or compromised.*

---

### 🖥️ [HavenLink-OS](https://github.com/RebelGuard/havenlink-OS)
A **hardened operating system** purpose-built for the HavenLink security suite. Stripped down, locked down, and ready for deployment in the field.

> *Because your OS is your first line of defense.*

---

### 🚁 [HavenLink-DroneGuard](https://github.com/RebelGuard/havenlink-DroneGuard)
**Passive RF-based drone detection** for at-risk teams, journalists, and activists. Monitors the airwaves for drone activity and alerts you instantly — all with HavenLink's privacy-first DNA.

> *Know when you're being watched from above.*
